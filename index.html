A Little About Me
My Process and Skills

I'm an experienced UX/UI designer with a drive to produce innovative media. I have the ability to analyze complex client needs, develop engaging solutions, and lead cross-functional teams. I'm passionate about designing seamless Human Computer Interactions.

Process

I'm a staunch user advocate. It is vital we remember we are building tools for people. I start my process by identifying who the target user is and what they are trying to accomplish. I do this by building user personas, use case scenarios, feature requests, and user flows.

The second phase in my process is visualizing solutions. I like to move quickly with sketches before moving into digital visualizations. I have been using Balsamic for wire-framing and I am deft with Photoshop and the Adobe Creative Suite.

Once the design and documentation are in place, development becomes easier. I am technically proficient in HTML, CSS and Javascript, and can use these skills to bring my designs to life. I am motivated by seeing my designs actualized.

Usability design should be iterative. Testing and user feedback generates a loop that can continually hone a design.

Orchard Software
Enterprise Level LIS

For the last three years I have been working exclusively with Orchard Software. I designed a desktop application for an Enterprise Level Laboratory Information System. I was a member of the R&D Team from the initial design to the sale of the product.

Hi-Fi Deliverables and Interaction Design

I'm fast with new ideas. I like to work quickly with sketches and whiteboard until I find visualizations that stick. Then I massage those ideas into Hi-Fi visuals, annotated images, and demonstrable interactions so development can move forward.

Designing Hi-Fi visuals helps facilitate communication between stakeholders and implementation teams.

I like using visuals to direct conversations about products. This allows communication to be concise and gives everyone involved shared context.

In my latest role as Human Computer Interaction Designer, I created visuals for every aspect of an Enterprise Level Desktop Application, from dashboards to control states. I also documented these decisions in a comprehensive style guide for further reference and future development.

The examples of the work contained on this site are proximities of the work I produced for Orchard Software. These concepts were designed to be passed along to an internal development team, outsourced to contractors, or personally implemented.
